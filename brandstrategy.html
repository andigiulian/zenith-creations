The Process

Every project is unique, but they all follow a structure to insure we create something great together.

Here's a basic outline descriping how we work and what you can expect from us.

Research & Discovery

Perhaps the most time consuming but vital step. In this stage we learn everything there is to learn about the business, helping us establish a better understanding of the brand's personality and purpose. In this stage we will research and identify many questions, such as who's the audience? The current state of the brand? Who's the competition? And so on.

Brain Storm

Once we have all the data and information in front of us, we take all that information an dtranslate the text and number based information into visual concepts and color schemes. We'll brainstorm and lay out multiple possibilities to ensure the designed elements are backed by the research and trigger pleasant emotions for the audience.

Production

In this stage, we'll take the approved concepts that best identify the brand, and go on to finalize the visuals, such as the logo design, logo layouts, color palette, typography, art style, etc. We'll make sure everything works together perfectly and reflects your brand in a beautiful and unique fashion.

Style Guide

At last we will pile all the designs and concepts we established and structure them into an easy-to-follow style guide so that your beautifully designed brand can keep on being followed and will never be used incorrectly.
