The Process

Every project is unique, but they all follow a structure to insure we create something great together.

Here's a basic outline descriping how we work and what you can expect from us.

Discovery

In the discovery phase we examine the needs and potential goals of the final product. We'll dive into in depth research to help you identify things such as the target audience, and/or essential features. Our team will then work on a list of possible solutions and structural plans.

Design

The design phase will come with mock up images of the website on exactly how it's going to look. We lay out each page of the project across different device sizes to ensure a pleasant user experience to every user before development.

Development

Once designs are approved, we will then work with out in-house developer to build the website and ensure everything from design to functionality is as described.

Launch

After development phase has been approved, the site will then be launched for your customers to enjoy. We offer regular management services for your website to ensure it is routinely up to date and free of bugs and errors.
