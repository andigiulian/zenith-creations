The Process

Every project is unique, but they all follow a structure to insure we create something great together.

Here's a basic outline descriping how we work and what you can expect from us.

Strategy

The idea is the start of every project. In this phase we will discuss and collaborate with you to solidify the visions and goals. From there our team will start strategic research and form a plan of action.

Pre-production

The phase is the set-up phase for everything that's yet to come. For videos, here is where we structure and solidify things such as scripts and storyboards. For photography projects, this could be where we secure locations or equipment and supplies.

Production

Filming, photography, animation and VFX all happen here. From rough draft, to edits, to polish, we make sure our partners are updated on a timely schedule to ensure everything is the way they envisioned.

Release

Once you are happy with the product, we'll wrap it up and send it your way!
