Web Design + Photography

An updated website alongside beautifully executed photography to reflect a historically enrighing shop and museum

The History Company

Your One Stop History Shop

The History Company came to us hoping to acquire an updated website in order to not only provide a more user friendly platform for their customers, but also become more easily discoverable by potential new customers.

Strategy

We took a trip to The History Company to talk to the owners and get a feel for the atmosphere and business style of this unique store. The shop was a history enthusiast's dream, filled with vast amounts of collections and uniforms. We decided the best way to showcase this shop's charm is a simple an straightforward visual design, and let the shop itself shine through with well placed photography reflecting the experiences one can find when they pay a visit.

Website

The website needed to be done on a short schedule, and needed to be simple to operate in order for the owner to list his shop items manually on a regular basis without needing to spend his time through a learning curve. Since the website needed to function as an additional ecommerce store, we chose to operate with Shopify, and provided the client a detailed instruction sheet on how to independently manage their website to them time and money.

Photography

We identified The History Company's charms as all located within the store itself, the best way to show off the shop and museum was to give customers a visual tour of the shop through still images, edited in a matching yellow color scheme with the purpose surfacing feelings of nostalgia, warmth, and curiosity.
